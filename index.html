About
This game is to practice typing numbers into a 10-key numpad, or onto a regular keyboard, too.

Each round is one minute.
To move, press the number on the tile next to the hero to move to that tile.
Navigate your way to tiles containing evil alien pigs, and press ENTER to slay them for points.

See how many points you can get in one minute!
