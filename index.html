Pigtolis Attacks...
Evil pigs from the Pigtopolis Kingdom have invaded the countryside of Numnia Kingdom!

As Numnia Kingdom's sole hero, you must repel the invasion by defeating all attacking pigs. Fortunately, you carry the famous hammer Numcalibur, providing you with invulnerability against the pigs' attacks.


The pigs cower and frighten at the sight of you, so slay them all!!

How to Play
To move, press the number on the tile next to the hero to move to that tile.
Navigate your way to tiles containing pigs, and press ENTER to slay them for points.

About
NumWarrior aims to improve your number-typing efficiency. By repeatedly keying in numbers at a fast pace, your fingers will remember where each number is on your keyboard.

As you keep trying to beat your previous high score, you get faster and more accurate!
